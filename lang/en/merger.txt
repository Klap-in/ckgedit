This version of [[doku>plugin:ckgedit|ckgedit]] has two significant changes:
  - It upgrades the CkEditor from 4.7 to 4.9, which gives up-to-date and improved javascript.
  - It integrates [[doku>plugin:ckgdoku|ckgdoku]] into ckgedit. This makes available to ckgedit the native DokuWiki Media Manager and linkwiz. Please see ckgdoku's [[doku>plugin:ckgdoku#configuration|configuration options]]

Please report any problems to  [[https://github.com/turnermm/ckgedit/issues|ckgedit's issues]] on github.
